Our Care

At Devine Housing Solutions, our mission is to provide affordable, supportive, and dignified housing for individuals in need across Columbia, SC and surrounding communities. We believe housing is more than shelter — it is the foundation for stability, recovery, and personal growth.

Safe & Affordable

We prioritize safe, clean, and affordable housing options so individuals can focus on rebuilding their lives without the stress of financial barriers.

Supportive Environment

Our homes are designed to create a supportive atmosphere that encourages stability, accountability, and community connections.

Dignity & Respect

Every individual we serve deserves dignity, respect, and the opportunity to thrive. Our housing programs reflect this core value.

Focused on Individuals

We serve seniors, veterans, recovery populations, re-entry individuals, low-income residents, and people with special needs — with programs tailored for individual success.
