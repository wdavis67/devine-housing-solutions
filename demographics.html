Demographics We Serve

We proudly provide affordable, all-inclusive housing for individuals across Columbia, SC and beyond. Our goal is to reduce homelessness and provide dignity, stability, and safety for every resident we serve.

Seniors (55+)

Safe, affordable housing for older adults seeking stability and comfort.

Veterans

Housing that honors those who served, with dignity and respect.

Recovery

Supportive homes for individuals rebuilding their lives in sobriety.

Re-Entry

Housing for individuals transitioning back into the community after incarceration.

Low-Income

Affordable, all-inclusive housing for individuals facing financial hardship.

Special Needs

Accessible homes for individuals with disabilities or other special needs.
