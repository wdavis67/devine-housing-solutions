Demographics We Serve

Providing affordable, all-inclusive housing for individuals in need across Columbia, SC and beyond.

Seniors (55+)

Safe, stable, and affordable housing designed for older adults who want comfort, dignity, and a supportive environment as they age.

Veterans

Housing that honors those who have served, providing stability, respect, and support for veterans transitioning into affordable living.

Recovery

Supportive homes for individuals working to rebuild their lives in sobriety, offering stability and an environment of growth.

Re-Entry

Housing opportunities for individuals transitioning back into the community after incarceration, helping reduce homelessness and recidivism.

Low-Income

Affordable, all-inclusive housing for individuals facing financial challenges, ensuring access to a safe and dignified living space.

Special Needs

Accessible housing for individuals with disabilities or unique needs, ensuring comfort, safety, and inclusion in a supportive community.
