Demographics Served

At Devine Housing Solutions, we proudly provide affordable housing and supportive living opportunities for individuals across Columbia, SC and the surrounding Midlands. Our programs focus on meeting the needs of diverse populations who require safe, stable, and dignified housing.

Seniors (55+)

Affordable, safe, and supportive housing designed specifically for seniors seeking stability and community.

Veterans

Providing affordable housing for those who have served our country, with a focus on dignity and respect.

Recovery

Housing solutions for men and women in recovery and sober living programs, promoting wellness and accountability.

Re-Entry Populations

Safe, supportive housing for individuals transitioning from incarceration, helping them reintegrate successfully into the community.

Low-Income Individuals

Affordable housing opportunities for those facing financial hardship, ensuring access to safe living environments.

Special Needs Residents

Accessible housing options for individuals with disabilities and special needs, designed for comfort and inclusivity.
