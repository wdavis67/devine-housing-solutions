Our Mission

At Devine Housing Solutions, our mission is to reduce homelessness and provide dignity, stability, and hope through safe and supportive housing. We serve individuals including seniors, veterans, those in recovery, returning citizens, low-income residents, and people with special needs.

All-Inclusive Housing

Our homes are fully furnished, with utilities included, and located in safe neighborhoods with easy access to transportation and community resources. We ensure a welcoming environment where every resident feels supported and valued.

Referrals Accepted Nationwide

While we are based in Columbia, SC, we accept referrals from across the United States. Case managers, agencies, and healthcare providers can connect individuals in need of affordable, supportive housing with us today.
