About Devine Housing Solutions

Devine Housing Solutions is an affordable housing provider in Columbia, SC. We are committed to creating safe, dignified, and supportive homes for individuals including seniors, veterans, those in recovery, re-entry populations, low-income residents, and people with special needs. Our mission is to help individuals find stability and community through housing.
