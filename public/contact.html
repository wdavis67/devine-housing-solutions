Important Care-Level Information

Devine Living Solutions is a non-medical group home. We serve adults whose needs fall within a low to moderate level of care.

We do not provide 24-hour skilled nursing, emergency medical supervision, or continuous medical care.

Outside medical, behavioral, therapeutic, or case-management services must be coordinated through qualified third-party providers.
