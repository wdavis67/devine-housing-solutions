Our Care Approach

Our supportive housing approach is centered on dignity, respect, stability, independence, safety, and community.

Respectful and Supportive Living

Devine Living Solutions provides a structured residential environment for adults who can live safely in a shared, non-medical group-home setting.

Dignity and Respect

Residents are treated with compassion, consideration, privacy, and respect for their individual experiences and needs.

Stability

A dependable home environment can help residents establish routines, maintain housing, and work toward personal goals.

Independence

Residents are encouraged to maintain independence and participate in daily life to the fullest extent appropriate for them.

Safety

Every referral is reviewed to determine whether the prospective resident can live safely within the home’s level of support.

Community

Shared living encourages connection, belonging, cooperation, healthy routines, and respectful relationships.

Collaboration

We communicate with families, case managers, social workers, and outside providers when appropriate and authorized.

Individual Placement Review

Every prospective resident is considered individually. Referral information helps us determine whether the home can safely and appropriately meet the person’s housing needs.

Factors We Consider

  • Current housing needs
  • Level of care
  • Mobility
  • Safety considerations
  • Household compatibility
  • Funding or payment arrangements
  • Outside support services
  • Current availability

Coordination With Outside Providers

Residents may receive medical, behavioral, therapeutic, social-work, case-management, or home-health services from qualified third-party providers.

Outside services are arranged separately and are subject to the resident’s eligibility, provider availability, and funding.

Care-Level Limitations

Devine Living Solutions is a non-medical group home. We are designed to serve adults whose needs fall within a low to moderate level of care.

We do not provide 24-hour skilled nursing, continuous medical monitoring, emergency medical supervision, or hospital-level care.

A prospective resident whose needs exceed the support available in the home may require a different type of placement.

Is Devine Living Solutions the Right Fit?

Submit a referral or contact our team to discuss the prospective resident’s needs, care level, support services, and placement options.
