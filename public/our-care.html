Our Care & Resident Support

We provide supportive, non-medical group living in well-maintained homes, with access to community resources and qualified outside service providers.

Supportive Homes in Established Communities

Our well-maintained homes are located in safe, established neighborhoods with access to transportation, community resources, schools, shopping, recreation, and other nearby services.

Each residence is structured to support the particular population it serves and to create a comfortable, respectful, and welcoming living environment.

Support Available Through Outside Providers

Depending on eligibility, need, funding, and provider availability, residents may receive services from independent third-party professionals.

Doctor & Nurse Home Visits

Qualified outside providers may offer in-home visits to support ongoing medical needs.

Home Health Services

Residents may receive RN, physical therapy, occupational therapy, or speech therapy services from outside providers.

Durable Medical Equipment

Equipment may be coordinated through qualified providers when medically necessary and approved.

Case Management

Case managers may help residents coordinate benefits, services, appointments, and community resources.

Social Work Support

Social workers may assist with transitions, planning, benefits, advocacy, and resource coordination.

Behavioral Counseling & Therapy

Residents may access qualified counseling or therapy providers when appropriate.

Medication Support

Outside providers may assist with medication routines, education, or management when approved and appropriate.

Additional Community Services

Other services may be available based on individual need, eligibility, and provider participation.

Our Supportive Living Approach

We focus on housing stability, comfort, structure, dignity, and connection.

  • Furnished common areas
  • Utilities, internet, and Wi-Fi
  • Washer and dryer access

  • Personal care welcome packet at move-in

What Outside Providers May Provide

  • Medical visits
  • Home health care

  • Behavioral health support
  • Case management
  • Social work services
  • Durable medical equipment
  • Medication support

Third-Party Service Disclaimer

Third-party services are not delivered directly by Devine Living Solutions. Availability depends on resident eligibility, provider approval, insurance, funding, scheduling, and service-area coverage.

Residents, families, case managers, and referral partners are responsible for coordinating and maintaining any outside medical or clinical services required by the resident.

Important: We Are a Non-Medical Group Home

Devine Living Solutions does not provide 24-hour skilled nursing, emergency medical supervision, or continuous medical care.

We accept adults whose needs fall within a low to moderate level of care and who can live safely in a shared, non-medical residential environment.

Each referral is reviewed to determine whether the resident’s needs are appropriate for the home and available support structure.

Have Questions About Care or Placement?

Contact our team or submit a referral so we can review the prospective resident’s needs and discuss placement suitability.
