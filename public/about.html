About Devine Housing Solutions

We provide supportive, all-inclusive housing in Columbia, South Carolina and surrounding areas including Lexington, Irmo, Cayce, West Columbia, Forest Acres, Sumter, Camden, Newberry, and Orangeburg.

Our Purpose

Devine Housing Solutions was created to address housing instability and reduce homelessness by offering safe, supportive living environments for individuals with diverse needs.

Our Commitment

Our homes are designed to provide dignity, structure, and access to coordinated care. We partner with agencies, case managers, and healthcare providers to support long-term stability.
