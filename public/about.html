About Us

Devine Housing Solutions is committed to restoring dignity through housing

Founded in Columbia, SC, Devine Housing Solutions was created to meet the need for affordable, all-inclusive homes for vulnerable individuals. Our team works to reduce homelessness by offering housing that includes utilities, furnishings, and access to supportive services. We partner with healthcare providers, case managers, and community organizations to ensure each resident has the resources they need to thrive.
