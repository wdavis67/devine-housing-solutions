About Us

Building dignity, reducing homelessness, and creating supportive housing solutions.

Serving Columbia, SC and Surrounding Areas

We provide supportive, all-inclusive housing in Columbia, South Carolina and surrounding areas including Lexington, Irmo, Cayce, West Columbia, Forest Acres, Sumter, Camden, Newberry, and Orangeburg.

Our Vision

We envision communities where everyone has access to safe, stable, and supportive housing. Devine Housing Solutions exists to serve individuals who are often overlooked by traditional housing markets, including seniors, veterans, individuals in recovery, returning citizens, low-income residents, and people with special needs.

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing well-maintained homes, supportive environments, and connections to services that promote independence and stability. Our focus is on dignity, respect, and helping residents move toward long-term stability and reduced risk of homelessness.
