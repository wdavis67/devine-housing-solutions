About Devine Living Solutions

We provide safe, supportive, all-inclusive living solutions for adults who benefit from structure, dignity, stability, and community connection.

Serving Columbia, South Carolina and Surrounding Communities

Devine Living Solutions serves adults throughout Columbia, South Carolina and nearby areas, including Lexington, Irmo, Cayce, West Columbia, Forest Acres, Sumter, Camden, Newberry, Orangeburg, and communities located within approximately one hour of Columbia.

We provide supportive, all-inclusive, non-medical group housing for adults who require low to moderate levels of assistance and who can safely live in a shared residential setting.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote dignity, stability, and opportunity through safe housing and supportive living environments.

Why We Exist

Devine Living Solutions was created to help reduce housing instability and homelessness by providing adults with clean, respectful, and structured places to live.

We work to create welcoming homes where residents can feel secure, build healthy routines, and maintain connections to community services.

Our Commitment

We are committed to maintaining comfortable homes, treating residents with respect, and working collaboratively with families, case managers, healthcare professionals, agencies, and community partners.

Each placement is considered carefully so that the home environment is appropriate for the resident’s needs and level of care.

Our Core Values

These principles guide how we serve residents, families, and referral partners.

Dignity

Every resident deserves to be treated with respect, compassion, and consideration.

Safety

We maintain clean, organized, and supportive living environments.

Stability

Our homes provide structure and consistency for adults seeking a more stable path.

Independence

We encourage residents to maintain appropriate independence and daily routines.

Collaboration

We work with families, agencies, and outside service providers to support successful placements.

Community

We value connection to local services, transportation, resources, and supportive relationships.

Important Care-Level Information

Devine Living Solutions is a non-medical group home. We provide housing for adults who need low to moderate levels of assistance.

We do not provide 24-hour skilled nursing or medical care. Residents may receive services from outside providers when appropriate, but those services are not delivered directly by Devine Living Solutions.
