Our all-inclusive approach provides residents with essential personal-care supplies, household necessities, home amenities, and flexible placement options.

Supportive, All-Inclusive Placement

Important: Our Level of Care

Devine Living Solutions is a non-medical group home. We accept adults whose needs fall within a low to moderate level of care.

We do not provide 24-hour skilled nursing or medical care. Residents must be able to live safely in a shared non-medical residential environment with appropriate outside support when necessary.

Placement suitability is reviewed carefully so that each resident’s needs are appropriate for the home and available support structure.

Outside Support Services

Depending on eligibility, individual needs, and provider availability, residents may receive services from independent third-party organizations.

These services may include home-health visits, case management, social-work support, behavioral counseling, therapy, durable medical equipment, medication-support coordination, and other community-based services.

Outside services are not delivered directly by Devine Living Solutions and may require separate provider approval or funding.
