Outside Community Services

Depending on eligibility and provider availability, residents may receive services from qualified independent providers.

These services may include home health, therapy, behavioral counseling, case management, social work, medication support, durable medical equipment, and other community-based assistance.

Non-Medical Housing

Devine Living Solutions is a non-medical group home. Our services are intended for adults with low to moderate support needs.

We do not provide 24-hour skilled nursing, emergency medical care, continuous medical monitoring, or hospital-level services.
