Services Included

Devine Housing Solutions provides supportive, all-inclusive short-term or permanent placement options. We are located in the Columbia, SC area and work with referral partners nationwide.

Personal Care Packet at Move-In

Every new resident receives a personal care packet at the time of move-in, which includes:

  • Toothbrush & toothpaste
  • Bar of soap
  • Lotion & deodorant
  • Lip balm
  • Hand sanitizer
  • Tissue
  • Nail clipper & nail file
  • Face cloth
  • Shampoo & conditioner

Monthly Home Amenities

Residents have access to the following amenities each month:

  • Washer and dryer access
  • TV in the community area
  • Internet and WiFi

Monthly Household Essentials Provided at the Home

These household essentials are provided monthly at the house:

  • Laundry detergent
  • Paper towels
  • Toilet paper
  • Trash bags
  • Cleaning supplies
  • Dishwashing liquid

All-Inclusive Placement Options

We provide all-inclusive housing options that can support:

  • Short-term placement
  • Permanent placement

We coordinate with referral partners to support appropriate placement and stability.

Important Care Level Note

We do not provide 24-hour medical care. Devine Housing Solutions is a non-medical group home.

We accept residents who are considered low to moderate care. If a resident requires higher-level medical care, we can discuss options and referral coordination to help find the appropriate setting.
