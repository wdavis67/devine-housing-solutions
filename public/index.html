Our Mission

Devine Housing Solutions exists to reduce homelessness by providing safe, stable, and all-inclusive housing for individuals who need supportive environments and dignity-centered care.

Who We Serve

We provide housing for seniors, veterans, individuals in recovery, returning citizens, low-income residents, and people with special needs.

Nationwide Referrals Accepted

Although our homes are located in the Columbia, SC area, we accept referrals from agencies and providers across the United States.
