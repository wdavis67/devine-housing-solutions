Serving Columbia, South Carolina and Nearby Communities

Devine Living Solutions provides supportive, all-inclusive housing in Columbia, South Carolina and surrounding areas, including Lexington, Irmo, Cayce, West Columbia, Forest Acres, Sumter, Camden, Newberry, Orangeburg, and communities within approximately one hour of Columbia.

We offer short-term and permanent placement options for adults who benefit from a clean, structured, respectful, and supportive living environment.

Important Care-Level Information

Devine Living Solutions is a non-medical group home. We provide supportive, all-inclusive housing for adults who require low to moderate levels of assistance.

We do not provide 24-hour skilled nursing or medical care. Prospective residents should be able to live safely in a non-medical group setting with appropriate outside support when needed.
