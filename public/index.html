Serving Columbia, SC and Surrounding Areas

We provide supportive, all-inclusive housing in Columbia, South Carolina and surrounding areas including Lexington, Irmo, Cayce, West Columbia, Forest Acres, Sumter, Camden, Newberry, and Orangeburg.

Our Mission

Devine Housing Solutions exists to reduce homelessness by providing safe, stable, and supportive housing for individuals who need dignity, structure, and long-term stability.

Who We Serve

We serve seniors, veterans, individuals in recovery, returning citizens, low-income individuals, and people with special needs. Referrals are accepted nationwide through agencies and case managers.
