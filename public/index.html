A Supportive Place to Call Home

Devine Living Solutions provides comfortable shared housing for adults with low to moderate support needs in Columbia, South Carolina and surrounding communities.

Safe and Stable Housing

Residents live in a structured, respectful, and welcoming residential environment designed to promote stability.

All-Inclusive Living

Utilities, internet, Wi-Fi, furnished shared spaces, household essentials, and laundry access are included.

Community Support

Residents may coordinate eligible medical, behavioral, therapeutic, and case-management services through outside providers.

Who We May Serve

We may consider seniors, veterans, adults with disabilities, individuals in recovery, returning citizens, low-income adults, and adults with special needs.

Every referral is reviewed individually for safety, compatibility, funding, availability, and level of care.

Learn Who We Serve

Important Care Information

Devine Living Solutions is a non-medical group home. We serve adults whose needs fall within a low to moderate level of care.

We do not provide 24-hour skilled nursing, emergency medical supervision, or continuous medical care.
