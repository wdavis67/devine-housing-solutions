Providing affordable, all-inclusive housing in Columbia, SC and beyond

Our Mission

We aim to reduce homelessness and provide dignity, stability, and hope through safe and supportive housing. Our homes serve seniors, veterans, those in recovery, returning citizens, low-income residents, and people with special needs.

All-Inclusive Housing

Our homes are fully furnished, utilities included, and located in safe neighborhoods with easy access to transportation and community resources. Residents enjoy comfort and support from day one.

Referrals Accepted Nationwide

Although based in Columbia, SC, we welcome referrals from across the United States. Case managers, agencies, and healthcare providers can connect individuals in need of affordable housing with us.
