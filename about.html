About Us

Devine Housing Solutions was founded with the mission of providing affordable, all-inclusive housing for individuals across Columbia, SC and surrounding areas. We are committed to creating homes that provide stability, dignity, and support for residents.

Our Mission

To ensure every individual has access to safe, stable housing — including seniors, veterans, recovery, re-entry, low-income, and special needs residents.

Our Vision

A future where affordable housing is available to all individuals in need, enabling them to live with dignity, stability, and community connection.

Why Choose Us

  • All-inclusive housing (rent, utilities, furnishings, essentials)
  • Focus on individuals, not families
  • Supportive and safe living environments
  • Referrals accepted nationwide
