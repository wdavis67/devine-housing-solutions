About Us

Devine Housing Solutions was founded with a mission to provide affordable housing for individuals in Columbia, SC and surrounding communities. We believe every person deserves a safe, stable, and supportive home environment, regardless of their background or financial situation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve lives by offering housing solutions for seniors, veterans, individuals in recovery, re-entry populations, low-income residents, and individuals with special needs. By providing more than just shelter, we help individuals build a foundation for stability, dignity, and growth.

Our Vision

We envision a community where every individual has access to affordable, safe housing and the opportunity to thrive. Through partnerships and compassionate care, we aim to create stronger, healthier communities across the Midlands.

Why Choose Us

  • Commitment to affordability and accessibility
  • Focus on individuals, not families — ensuring tailored support
  • Programs designed to serve diverse demographics
  • A passion for community impact and long-term change
